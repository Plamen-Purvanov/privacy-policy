Privacy Policy

Trade Tycoon: Wall Street
Last updated: March 2, 2026

This privacy policy describes how NovaBit Games ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game Trade Tycoon: Wall Street ("the Game").

Information We Collect

The Game itself does not require any user accounts, registration, or personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

However, the Game uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically:

Third-Party Services

Unity Ads — We use Unity Ads to display advertisements in the Game. Unity Ads may automatically collect the following types of data:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating system, screen size)
  • IP address
  • General location data (country/region level, not precise location)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Unity Technologies in accordance with their privacy policy. You can review Unity's privacy policy at: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Local Data Storage

The Game stores your game progress (such as score, level, and settings) locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system. This data is not transmitted to any server and remains solely on your device. Uninstalling the Game will delete this data.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The third-party advertising services used in the Game may have their own policies regarding children's data.

Your Choices

You can control ad personalization through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

You can also reset your Advertising ID or delete your game data by uninstalling the Game.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of data breach from our side. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
