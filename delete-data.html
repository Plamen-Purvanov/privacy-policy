Delete Your Data

Trade Tycoon: Wall Street
Developer: NovaBit Games

Trade Tycoon: Wall Street does not collect or store any personal data on external servers. All game data is stored locally on your device. Below are the steps to delete your data.

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1: Open your device Settings.
Step 2: Go to Apps (or Applications).
Step 3: Find and tap Trade Tycoon: Wall Street.
Step 4: Tap Storage, then tap Clear Data. This will delete all your game progress and settings.
Step 5: Alternatively, uninstall the app to permanently remove all data from your device.

To Reset Your Advertising ID

Step 1: Open device Settings.
Step 2: Go to Google > Ads.
Step 3: Tap Reset Advertising ID or Delete Advertising ID.

Types of Data

Data Type Storage On Clear/Uninstall
Game progress (score, level, settings) Local device only Permanently deleted
Game preferences Local device only Permanently deleted
Advertising ID Device system Reset via device settings

No data is retained after uninstallation. We do not store any data on external servers, so there is no additional retention period.
